Double Cleansing

Inspired by the homeopathic theory that oil attracts and eliminates oil, Japanese women centuries ago began to double cleanse as the only method to remove the metallic white makeup that was popular at the time. They massaged their faces with an emollient oil, before washing it thoroughly with a foam-based cleanser. In the process, they discovered that double cleansing did more than just remove makeup, it nourished and hydrated their skin.
